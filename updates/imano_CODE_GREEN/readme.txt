	Following a large-scale otherworldly event, the entirety of a desolate seaside town is altered overnight. Buildings
	disappear and reappear overnight, signs distort, and strange creatures start walking amongst humans in the streets. The 
	townspeople, however, seem to not have noticed; or if they did, they made no indication that anything is out of the 
	ordinary. And worse, the more information about the phenomena spreads, the wider the range of anomalous activities become.
	
	Resolution? Put all affected areas on lockdown.
	
	Despite the panic-inducing circumstances, human curiosity cannot be quenched. And so, in an attempt to understand the 
	unknown, the newly formed New-world Anomaly Observation and Research Institution has sent in field agents, masquerading 
	as the town's residents, to record the town's strange existence. IMANO, a newly graduated Institution trainee, is one 
	such agent. You are her operator, a helping hand for the Institution in charge of overseeing her activities. Being a 
	field agent isn't simple work; helping one wouldn't be either. But then, she might just make things a lot easier.
